Venue Requirements

Climate Control - Critical

Strong air conditioning is essential (21°C target, 4000 m³/min air renewal). Participants wear full overalls, gloves, and masks for 75 minutes. The venue must maintain comfortable temperatures throughout.

Venue Space

Experience: 20-26m x 14-18m (by capacity), Changing: 250-350m², plus lobby, control room (15m²), and storage. Total footprint: ~500-1000m².

View detailed floor plan →

Ceiling Height

Optimal 10-12 meters. Dark gray perimeter frame must be at least 5m high. Projectors mounted on trusses for 360° projection mapping.

Full Blackout

Complete light control required. No external light can enter the experience space during shows. Essential for projection quality.

Sound System

360-degree surround sound system (4.1 or 2.1 minimum). Clear voice narration and immersive audio are central to the experience.
