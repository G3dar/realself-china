Video System

360° projection mapping on all 4 walls and floor. Target: 100 ANSI lumens per m² on dark gray surface.

Experience Room
  • 26 projectors (6,500 - 12,000 ANSI lumens)*
  • 26 angular lenses*
  • 26 outputs Disguise or Watchout video server
Changing Room
  • 1 projector (5,000 - 7,000 ANSI lumens)
  • 1 normal or angular lens
  • 1 projection screen 5m x 3m
  • 1 video server / 1 HD output / audio out

The choice of projector power and lenses depends on venue conditions. We will work with your team to review your local equipment proposal and ensure the best possible experience.
