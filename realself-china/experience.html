The Experience

A journey to the unknown within

The world bombards us with stimuli constantly. Every notification, every image, every demand for our attention pulls us further from ourselves.

Real Self brings you closer to connect with the one who perceives those stimuli. Not the roles you play, not the masks you wear in daily life, but the one who watches, who feels, who simply is.

The mind is like water that bubbles, constantly creating and emitting thoughts. This project is like a blank page where you simply pour out the sensations you have and can see them with more clarity.

Phase I

The Unknown

Entering an uncharted territory

From the moment you abstract yourself from who you are, you enter an unknown space, an uncharted territory.

You don't know what's going to happen. The voice guides you to places you don't usually visit. It feels like a journey to something unknown where you don't know what the voice will tell you next.

Everyone wears the same.
Everyone is anonymous.
Something extraordinary becomes possible.

Phase III

Reconnection with Others

The revelation, the celebration

A journey that rescues energy, and that energy then explodes in the moment of happiness, in the moment of the party, in the moment of the revelation.

A journey that goes from the unknown to a reconnection with oneself and then a reconnection with the other.

The masks come off. You see faces for the first time. And you realize you've shared something profound with strangers who are no longer strangers.
