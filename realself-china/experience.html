The Experience

120 minutes of transformation

Real Self is an immersive theatrical experience that has captivated audiences in Buenos Aires, Barcelona, and Santiago. Participants wear identical white overalls, gloves, and masks, creating complete anonymity that liberates authentic human connection. We're excited to bring this transformative experience to China and expand into new territories.

Duration

120 minutes total (55 min experience + 65 min transitions)

Capacity

150-350 participants per session

Technical Requirements for China

Climate Control - Important

Strong air conditioning required. Costumes are very warm - participants wear full overalls, gloves, and masks for 55+ minutes.

Venue Space

150 pax: Experience 210m² + Changing 160m². 350 pax: Experience 480m² + Changing 350m². Plus foyer, storage, and control areas.

Ceiling Height

Minimum 6 meters, ideal 9 meters for floor projection. Projectors mounted on ceiling with full floor coverage.

Full Blackout

Complete light control required. No external light can enter the experience space during shows.

Technical specifications will be coordinated with your local AV production team during the setup phase.
